About the show

Business building, no fluff.

Managed Chaos is a business podcast built for leaders navigating the gap between a great idea and a company that actually runs. Each episode is about 15 minutes long, designed to give you actionable things you can implement today.

From IP protection to go-to-market strategy and everything in between, we have an episode for you. Hosted by Katrina Purcell, fractional COO and founder of Katrina Purcell LLC.

Your host
Katrina Purcell

Katrina Purcell is a Columbia Business School graduate with over 20 years of experience in B2B technology. She drives organizational transformation by leveraging her expertise in process optimization, SOPs, and change management. As the founder of Katrina Purcell LLC, a growth operations consultancy, she partners with ambitious tech startups and nonprofits, delivering strategic guidance and tailored solutions that unlock their full potential.
